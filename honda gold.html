Description

Honda Gold Wing is one of the most expensive bikes that are currently present in the Indian market. It has an efficient engine enabling a comfortable riding experience. There have been many variants of the Honda Gold Wing, however, the launch of the first model traces back to the year 1981. The bike comprises many features and has always been marketed as a premium motorcycle to cater to the elite classes. Honda chose to incorporate the body design of a cruiser motorcycle to support the intricacies of this motorcycle. The extensive and heavy frame helps the riders to have better control which in turn results in a comfortable riding experience. The company launched two variants of the motorcycle for distinctive purposes. The primary model is the Honda Gold Wing whereas the special edition is called the Honda Gold Wing Tour. Both these models gauged a wide audience that resulted in a high quantum of sales of the bike..

The seating capacity of the bike is capped at two people, however, most riders use it to ride solo as the kerb weight of the bike already exceeds 350 kilograms. In terms of longer routes, Honda Gold Wing is a decent bike that is efficient in fulfilling its purpose. Most people have a misconception that the bike is difficult to ride but this notion is false. It takes a few days for the rider to get accustomed to the design before they can ride the bike effortlessly. Many riders face the problem of balancing the bike but this issue fades away with a bit of focus and concentration..

The Honda Gold Wing includes a liquid-cooled 1833 cc engine that offers the riders to experience a maximum speed of 180 kmph with a top-notch torque of 170 Nm at 7,000 rotations per minute. The Honda Gold Wing is a stellar offering by Honda that includes every possible feature present in premium class bikes. It does not offer good mileage but its premium nature compensates for this shortcoming..
